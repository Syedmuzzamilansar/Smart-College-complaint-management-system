About the College

Established in 1985 • Government of Telangana

Quli Qutub Shah Government Polytechnic was established by G.O. Ms No. 255 on 10th July 1985. It was initially started with three branches — DCE, DME and DECE — with an intake of 40 each and was located in a private building at Jahanuma. It was subsequently shifted to another private building at Chandrayangutta. Later on 20th November 1992, it was shifted to its permanent building at Chandulal Baradari, Near Zoo Park, Old City, Hyderabad.

Vision & Mission

Our guiding principles • Building the future

Our Vision

To empower and produce skilled and competent individuals with good personal attributes from Old City in Hyderabad of Telangana State by imparting required knowledge and skills to meet the global proficiency.

Our Mission

  • To create conducive learning environment for trending interactive teaching-learning process.
  • To inculcate updated practical-oriented learning through the interaction of industry-institute to meet the needs of the industries.
  • To produce technically skilled individuals with professionalism, responsibility and ethical values.
